Pulparindo

Pulparindo

Puparindo is a chewy candy made from tamarind pulp. It is spicy, sour and sweet

This comes in 3 different flavors. The original, watermelon and mango. This treat it perfect for those who love sweet and sour and aren't afraid of a little bit of spice.

Pelon

pelon

This candy is one of the most popular candies in Mexico it is also made from tamarind pulp but has a fun twist to it.

This candy comes in small tube that you can squeeze the candy out from. When you squeeze it out it looks like strands of hair coming out the top. This candy also has a bit of spice to it

Picafresa

Picafresa

Picafresa is strawberry flavored gummy covers in chilipowder and sugar. It is tiny but does pack a punch of flavor.

If you like strawberry flavors but would like to try something with a kick this is your candy.

Bubulubu

Bubulubu

Bubulubu is a chocolate bar with a filling. The outer layer is chocolat but the inside is marshmallow witha strawberry jam center. Many people in Mexico like to eat this chocolate bar chilled.

If you are a fan of chocolate and marshmallow you can't miss out on Bubulubu.

Carlos V

Carlos V

Carlos V has been a very well known chocolate bar. Named after a King from spain who introduced chocolate to the courts in Europe. This choclate is delicious, smooth and milky.

This delicous treat will melt in your mouth. If you're crazy about chocolate this is a bar that you have to try.

Rebanaditas

Rebanaditas

Rebanaditas are a watermelon flavored lolipop covered in chili powder. This is a very popular lolipop in Mexico. They are called rebanaditas or "little slices" in English because the resemble a watermelon slice in shape.

These lolipops are a lot of fun once you get through the spice a delicous watermelon flavored candy awits beneath.
