Mazapan

Mazapan

Mazapan is a peanut based candy it is very sweet and crumbly.

It's a small but flavorfull sweet. It's soft and melts in your mouth.

If you want something small yet sugary and filling this is the treat for you!

Dulce de Leche

Dulce de Leche

Dulce de leche is a milk based caramel. When made with goat's milk it's called cajeta.It's creamy and has a unique flavor

This a widely popular sweet know through out central american and Mexico. Often eaten with churros or used to fill breads or as a topping in many desserts

This treat has many uses and if you love to add a little extra sweetness to any desert this is for you!

Cocada

Cocada

Cocadas are delicious treats. They are made with shredded coconut and condensed milk.

In Mexico you can often find this in the 3 colors of the Mexican flag. The more traditional cocads you will find in the form of a ball

If you are a fan of coconut and condensed milk then this is the treat for you!

Ate

Ate

Ate is a very traditional candy. Ate has spanish roots. This candy was brought over from spain.

Ate is made from real fruit. These fruit are then processed into a thick jelly this is why ate has a chewy consistency.

A really popular flavor is Guayaba (Guava) but there are many fruit flavored candies.

If you love fruits and exotic flavors this is the candy for you!

Obleas

Obleas

Obleas are very thin wafers that are stacked on top of each other and usally have a dulce de leche or cajeta filling in the middle.You will often see them come in various colors.

They made with wheat flour sugar and oil. Obleas are delious they melt in your mouth and have a bit of a crunch to them.

If you're looking for a light, yet sweet snack this is a great choice!

Borrchitos

Borrachitos

Borrachitos are candy with a chewy and jelly consitency and they ar covered with cryslized sugar sprinkles. They are called borrachitos because they contain alcohol.

These candies normally come in a pack with a variety of flavors like pinaapple,eggnog,strawberry or lemon.

If you like candies with a gummy texture and with little kick to them this is the treat for you!
